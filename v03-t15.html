V.03 - T15

Bikram Sutar October 4, 2024

Everything changes. Change is the only constant. You either try hard to drive the change, try controling it or flow with the change. You have no other choice.

Not every change is worth your attention. But some are. Some are worth everything. If I could I would put all the resources I could imagine. If it feels like this, and if it is realistic. Be optimistic about it and put everything in it. Control the change. 

I have been controling my change. Okay, 'Fate' - here I say the magic word. Does this mean I control everything? No, I surely can only control a very insignificant of part of this big game called Life. But this insignificant small part plays a huge part in determining my fate. If fate is the infintely running car that I can't control speed of, I can certainly control steering at times. Giving me enough control to pass by certain vallies. 

I don't like to sit static and react to change. I want to proactive. I want to guide them. I can't control the most part of it. But some vallies I want to visit, I will do everything to steer the engine it to take me there. It is time to steer it again. To new direction. To new valley.
