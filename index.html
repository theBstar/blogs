Hey, Welcome 👋 

I am sharing my journey here. Many of these are just self reflections, introspection notes. I used to write regularly around 2019. Then I got too comfortable on my whatsapp notes. But nothing can beat the thrill of publishing on internet right? So here I am.

Life has been good to me. And I believe eventually it will be good to everyone. Super highs, lows, everything will be averaged out. And collectively this average will only go up, and you will play an important role to take this average up - doesn't matter where you are, what you are doing, you will make the life good. 

I think this is a good glimpse into what I will do here. Let's go ✌️
