T - 20 🚀

Bikram Sutar October 1, 2024

No, I am not referring to T20 cricket by T-20 here. I am not that mad about cricket anymore. Not mad about watching cricket anymore. It was 2012, I used to be mad about watching a game. I still play it. I enjoy playing a lot. Infact, Cricket, Running and writting are three passive meditations for me. 

I just saw the scores for ongoing Bangladesh vs India match. India playing test match like a T20. Trying to force a result. Forcing a result and not being content with a draw is a new behaviour, these top boards are pushing for after WTC cycle started. Test match wins have higher prize associated, more than the mere series win earlier. A higher prize, a greater value is what driving the behaviour change. 

Yes, Change! Mark this word. Something is changing. Something in my life. Well, I can't open it to you now. I can only say - T-20 is the count down. Nicely wraped in cricket flavour. 

T-20 🚀
