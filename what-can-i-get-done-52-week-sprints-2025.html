What can I get done (52-week sprints) - 2025

Bikram Sutar January 20, 2025

Elon acquired Twitter, renamed it to x.com, and laid off a significant workforce, yet it continues to strive more efficiently. This one question from him doing rounds on x.com - 'What did you get done this week?'. By asking this every week, I can infuse the urgency. The answer to this should be directional. There should be a goal I am working towards every week. This is a living document of 52 sprints.  

Here is the direction - 

  1. Leverage - Wealth, brand. Questions to answer - 
    • How much money did I make?
    • How many people did I reach?
    • How many valuable connections did I make?
  2. Health - Let's have the visible abs this year. If not this year it isn't happening anytime. Questions to answer
    • What did I do this week to have visible abs?
    • Did I sleep well? Oversleep, under-sleep?
  3. Knowledge - Be on top of AI and establish engineering excellence in the network. Math is back. 
    • What did I learn?
    • What did I build & publish on the web? Engineering Article, new product, open source contribution?
    • What did I read?
    • What did I learn in math?

I will pursue items from this list, but all of them are secondary, and I should not be judged by them. I have a wishlist that includes playing plenty of cricket matches, achieving a new personal record for a half marathon, learning a language (which was my goal for 2024), learning to play a musical instrument, and writing more poems and sketches. However, I should not be judged by these pursuits. When the year ends, I want to review and check off the above three items. If I can check off anything from the wishlist, that will be a bonus. 

Let's start ⚡

 

Week 4 (Week of 20th Jan)

  1. Health - Resume Gym (have been off from mid-2024)
  2. Leverage -
    • Money - I have 1L to play around and generate a return from the market/crypto/physical investment. Set up a plan/schedule this week. So that I can start thinking/executing from next week. Can I grow it X% in 3 months (X to be filled)?
  3. Knowledge
    • Get back to reading AI.
    • Be on track to complete 11th math by next week.  

 

Week 1 to 3

I didn't get anything done in these 3 weeks that aligned with the direction I had set. I am here finalizing the direction on the 4th week, 20th of Jan. Hence, Can I say I created the clarity? Saying this, I did start re-reading the 11th Math book. Read Set theory, functions, and Relations.
